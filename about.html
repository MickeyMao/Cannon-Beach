Founded in 1947 by Daryll James, the Bent Spoke is a bike tour business. Since our opening we have beeen committed to giving our clients the ability to experience the beauty of Cannon Beach and the surrounding forests. We are always ready to answer any questions, and because of our history serving not only tourists, but also locals, we are ready to explain anything to you. We offer a variety of tours with varying degrees fo difficulty, from single day beach riding to multi-day camping and intense mountain biking tours, we have anything one might want. We are completely family owned since Grandpa James opened our doors. We welcome all who come through our doors. We hope to see you soon.
